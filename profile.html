Blockchain
A blockchain is, an immutable time-stamped series record of data that is distributed and managed by cluster of computers.
Token
Tokens are a representation of a particular asset or utility, that usually resides on top of another blockchain
Smart Contract
A smart contract, also known as a cryptocontract , is a computer program that directly controls the transfer of digital currencies or assets between parties under certain conditions.
